Customer Questions

What types of coffee do you offer?

We offer a variety of blends including espresso, arabica, cold brew, and more. All beans are ethically sourced

Do you deliver?

Yes, we deliver across the country with free shipping on orders above ₹ 30.

Can I subscribe for monthly coffee deliveries?

Yes! We offer customizable subscription plans with free shipping and member-only discounts.

What grind sizes do you offer?

We offer whole beans, coarse grind (for French press), medium (drip), and fine (espresso).

Are your coffee beans organic?

Yes, we source our beans from certified organic farms around the world.
